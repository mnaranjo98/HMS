About Hesma Sports

MORE THAN AN
AGENCY

Hesma Sports is a professional football representation agency committed to developing and positioning elite Ecuadorian talent on the global stage. We don't just negotiate contracts — we build careers, protect futures, and open doors that change lives.

International Network
Direct connections with clubs, scouts and directors across South America, Europe and beyond.
Player Protection
Legal, contractual and personal support at every stage of the player's career.
Career Development
Strategic planning to maximize each player's market value and on-field performance.

What We Do

OUR
SERVICES

01
Sports Representation
Professional representation in negotiations with clubs, federations and sports entities at national and international level.
02
Contract Negotiation
Expert contract management to ensure the best conditions for each player's short and long-term development.
03
Personal Branding
Building the player's public image, social media management and commercial sponsorship opportunities.
04
International Opportunities
Active scouting of international transfer opportunities and direct mediation with clubs in South America, Europe and the USA.
05
Talent Scouting
Detection and evaluation of emerging talent in Ecuadorian football to build the next generation of elite players.
06
Career Management
Strategic planning of the player's sports career from youth academies to the professional level, maximizing their potential.
07
Transfers & Loans
Management of permanent transfers, loans and free-agent processes with maximum value and security for the player.
08
Sponsorships
Negotiation and management of commercial agreements, sports brand deals and institutional sponsorships for players.
